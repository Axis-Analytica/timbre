# Your Voice Framework

This file is created during setup. It contains your writing voice rules, tone guidelines, and anti-patterns.

Edit this anytime through the Voice Guide view in the app, or directly in this file.

## Getting started

Describe your writing voice here. Think about:

- **Tone** — Are you formal or casual? Warm or direct? Academic or conversational?
- **Audience** — Who are you writing for? What do they care about?
- **Structure** — How do you typically open a piece? How do you end?
- **Anti-patterns** — What should your writing never sound like?
- **Signature moves** — What makes your writing recognisably yours?

The more specific you are, the better the AI can match your voice.
